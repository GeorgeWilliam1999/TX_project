# R1 Re-evaluation — 2026-05-20

## What changed
Phase R1 replaced the selection metric from **mean |Δx|** (old `abs_dx_mm` in `validate()`)
with **median |Δx|** and added the full quantile suite (p68/p95/p99). The training loss was
changed from normalised MSE to log-cosh for future runs; existing checkpoints were **not
retrained** — only re-evaluated.

## Key findings

### F1 — The previously reported "0.117 mm" was the MEAN, not the median
The old `validate()` computed `abs_dx / count` = arithmetic mean of |Δx|. With the corrected
median metric, `pinn_v2_small_v1` is at **11.72 µm median** — 10× better than the number quoted
in README.md §M1. The gen-3 §F2 heavy-tail diagnosis is confirmed: the mean was dominated by
< 1 % of outlier tracks.

### F2 — The project target "< 0.10 mm" is already met at median level
`pinn_v2_small_v1` at 11.72 µm median is **8× below the 100 µm target**. However:
- **p95 = 454 µm** — 5 % of tracks have errors > 4× the target. This is the heavy-tail population.
- **p99 = 1849 µm** — the worst 1 % are very poor (> 15× target).
- **pos_rms = 646 µm** — the RMS is driven entirely by the tail.

The target "< 0.10 mm" must be clarified: if it means *median*, the model already passes. If it
means *mean* or *RMS*, it does not. **Recommendation: adopt median as the primary figure of merit
and p95 as the quality gate.** A model with median 12 µm and p95 < 200 µm would be an excellent
track extrapolator; p95 < 100 µm would be world-class.

### F3 — MLPs are completely collapsed (confirmed)
`mlp_medium_v1_broken` at 3684 µm median, `mlp_small_v1_broken` at 11 818 µm median.
Both are useless as extrapolators. R3 (MLP modernisation with 7-dim input) is still required
before MLPs can be compared to PINN_v2.

## Verdict
- `pinn_v2_small_v1`: **PASS on median target** — 11.72 µm << 100 µm.
  NEEDS INVESTIGATION on p95 (454 µm; tail reduction is the Phase R4 objective).
- MLPs: **FAIL** — proceed to Phase R3 (Fix M1: 7-dim input).
- Ordering robust across metric switch: PINN_v2 >> MLP_medium >> MLP_small (unchanged).

## Impact on plan
- R1 exit gate: **met** (orderings confirmed, quantiles published).
- R4 target revision: consider adding p95 < 200 µm as a secondary gate alongside median < 100 µm.
- UPDATE `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md` and `REPLACEMENT_PLAN.md` to quote 11.72 µm (median) rather than
  0.117 mm (mean) for `pinn_v2_small_v1`.
